About Me

Hello, my name is Tony Domante, and I am on a journey to become a software developer.
My path has not been easy, as I have faced significant challenges throughout my life. Unlike many,
I do not have the luxury of pursuing higher education in a traditional manner.
However, I am determined to overcome these obstacles by becoming a self-taught programmer.
My aim is to secure a stable job that will allow me to support myself while pursuing my ultimate
goal of studying electrical engineering.

Becoming a software developer is only the first step in my larger vision. My passion lies in the
creation and development of technology that can improve the world and make it a safer place. I firmly
believe that to achieve this, it is essential to pair innovative hardware with equally well-designed
software. By mastering software development, I will be equipped to design and integrate the best
possible software solutions for the hardware products I aim to create in the future.

Although I am starting from scratch, I have found the learning process to be both challenging and
fulfilling. I already have several ideas in mind for projects that will help me build my developer
portfolio, and I am committed to refining my skills. This is just the beginning of my journey to
becoming a skilled engineer, and I look forward to using my talents to contribute to the betterment of
all mankind!
